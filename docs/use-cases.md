# Use Cases

## 1. Landing Page Evaluation
Analyze landing pages to identify messaging weakness, CTA clarity issues, and trust-related friction.

## 2. Conversion Barrier Detection
Detect possible reasons why users hesitate, abandon the page, or fail to take action.

## 3. Trust Signal Analysis
Evaluate visual and structural cues that influence perceived credibility and confidence.

## 4. Marketing Decision Support
Generate explainable outputs that help marketers and founders prioritize improvements.

## 5. Behavioral Interpretation
Translate user-facing design and content signals into actionable marketing intelligence.

## 6. AI-Assisted Reporting
Produce structured, human-readable summaries from technical and behavioral analysis layers.
